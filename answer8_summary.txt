8. Benefits and Limitations of Using a VPN:


Benefits:


Enhanced Privacy: Hides your real IP address, making online activities more anonymous.


Secure Data Transmission: Encrypts internet traffic, protecting sensitive information from potential eavesdroppers.


Access to Restricted Content: Allows bypassing of geo-restrictions and censorship by connecting to servers in different countries.


Protection on Public Wi-Fi: Secures connections on unsecured networks, reducing the risk of data breaches.




Limitations:


Reduced Internet Speed: Encryption and routing through distant servers can lead to slower connection speeds.


Limited Server Access: Free versions may offer fewer server locations compared to paid plans.


Potential Incompatibility: Some websites or services may block VPN traffic, limiting access.


Data Caps: Free plans might have data usage limits, restricting the amount of data that can be transferred.
